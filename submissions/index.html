Submission Details

To ensure your submission is considered, please adhere to the following guidelines:

  • Formatting Instructions: Use the following style files when preparing your submission. Papers need to be prepared and submitted as a single file: We accept long (8 pages) and short (4 pages) papers, with unlimited pages for references and appendix in both cases. The maximum size of submissions is 50 MB. While your submission can contain a supplement or appendix, please note that reviewers are not obliged to review supplementary material.
  • Reviews: The review process will be double-blind. All submissions must be anonymized and the leakage of any identification information is prohibited.
  • If your paper is accepted it will not appear in any proceedings, journal, or book. We will post the list of accepted papers on the workshop website.

To submit your work, please visit the OpenReview submission website.
